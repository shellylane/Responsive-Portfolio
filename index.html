About Me

Hi! I'm Shelly. I am a Fullstack (MERN) Web Developer in the Phoenix, AZ area. I recently finished the University of Arizona Coding Bootcamp in December, I am currently working as a teaching assistant for the next cohort of the same bootcamp I attended.
